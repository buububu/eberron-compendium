Vestments of the Trickster – Legendary Studded Leather

← Back to Light Armor

Overview

This shimmering vest was once worn by a changeling spy in the employ of House Phiarlan. It bears enchantments of misdirection and movement drawn from the Plane of Shadow.

Properties

  • AC 13 + Dex modifier, no stealth disadvantage
  • Advantage on Deception and Sleight of Hand
  • Once per short rest: Misty Step (no slot required)

Usage & Roleplay

The vest shimmers subtly and blends into surroundings, adapting to lighting and nearby colors.
