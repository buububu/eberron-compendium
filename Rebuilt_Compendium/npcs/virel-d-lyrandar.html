Captain Virel d'Lyrandar

Overview

Captain Virel is a Lyrandar airship commander operating in the skies of Khorvaire. Known for her bold maneuvers and loyalty to House Lyrandar, she commands the Thunderwake with a seasoned crew.

Role in the Story

Virel may serve as an ally, employer, or rival depending on party choices. She offers passage between cities, helps with aerial combat, or might oppose smugglers and pirates.

Affiliations

  • House Lyrandar

Notable Traits

  • Race: Half-Elf
  • Background: Noble
  • Known For: Tactical brilliance, airship duels
  • Personality: Stern, decisive, loyal to her house

Timeline Appearance

Modern Era only. She does not appear in earlier timelines.
