Bladeling of the Mournland

Overview

These twisted constructs roam the ruins of Cyre, born from metal, sorrow, and the lingering magic of the Mourning. Bladelings are composed of warped armor plates, jagged blades, and flickers of arcane static.

Stats (for DMs)

  • Type: Construct
  • CR: 3
  • HP: 55 (7d8 + 21)
  • AC: 17 (natural armor)
  • Speed: 30 ft.
  • Damage Resistances: Lightning, Psychic
  • Condition Immunities: Charmed, Exhaustion

Abilities

  • Blade Whirl (Recharge 5–6): Each creature within 10 ft. must make a DC 14 Dex save or take 4d6 slashing damage.
  • Arcane Echo: When damaged by magic, the bladeling gains +1 AC until the end of its next turn.

Habitat & Behavior

Found only within the Mournland, bladelings operate in small packs that protect cursed ruins and shattered fortresses. They exhibit no language, but appear to follow command echoes left by fallen Cyran war-mages.

Encounters

Commonly appear in quests tied to the Mournland or as guardians in “Silence of Swords”.
