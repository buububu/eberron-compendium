Althaea Duresyn

“Stormbound Seer of Sharn”

Race: Elf

Gender: Female

Age: 42 (Elf)

Description: Tall and slender with sharply defined features, silver hair cascading past the shoulders, and sea-gray eyes that shimmer faintly when storms gather. She bears an air of detached grace, as if forever half-listening to something only she can hear.

Faith & Affiliations

Religion: The Devourer

Affiliated Group: None (formerly a novice of a now-lost sea temple)

Connection: Althaea believes storms carry divine intent and cleansing chaos. Her devotion to the Devourer is rooted in awe, not fear.

Backstory

Origin: Sharn

Background: Acolyte

Personality: Reserved, intuitive, and cryptic. She often quotes storm omens and rarely smiles unless thunder is overhead.

Backstory: Born during a three-day storm above the cliffs of Sharn’s lower district, Althaea was marked from infancy by strange weather patterns. Raised by a fringe sect devoted to the Devourer, her talent for divine magic grew with each violent tempest. When the temple fell to sabotage during the Last War, she wandered alone into the Mournland and was changed by the wild magic within.

Special Ability: Years of listening to the winds have granted her the supernatural ability to interpret weather as prophecy.
Effect: Once per long rest, she may predict the weather for the next 24 hours with flawless accuracy, and gains advantage on Insight or Religion checks related to interpreting omens or divine signs.
