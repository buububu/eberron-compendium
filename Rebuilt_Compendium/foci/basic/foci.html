Spellcasting Foci

Spellcasting foci are arcane, divine, or primal tools used to channel magical energy. While often chosen for mechanical reasons, each type of focus carries cultural, religious, or historical significance across Eberron.

Wands

Description: Thin rods of enchanted wood, bone, or crystal. Common among wizards, artificers, and warlocks.

Classes: Wizard, Warlock, Artificer

Roleplay Use: Pointing a wand to trace arcane sigils or fire magical bolts. Some may whisper to the wand, or etch temporary glyphs midair.

Related Legendary Foci: Wand of the Stormcall

Orbs

Description: Smooth spheres of glass, crystal, or stone, often levitating slightly when active. Associated with divination and mental magic.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard

Roleplay Use: Used for gaze-based incantations, illusion shaping, or focusing psionic will into a clear surface.

Related Legendary Foci: Orb of the Dreaming Depths
