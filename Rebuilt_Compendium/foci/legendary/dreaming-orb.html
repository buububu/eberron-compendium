Orb of the Dreaming Depths – Legendary Focus

← Back to Orb Focus

Overview

This deep-blue orb is carved from a shard of Kythri stone and constantly shifts with dreamlike images. It once belonged to a kalashtar oracle who drowned in the thoughts of the Quori.

Properties

  • +2 spell attack & DC for Divination and Enchantment spells
  • Cast Dream or Detect Thoughts once/day without components
  • Immune to magical sleep

Usage & Roleplay

The orb pulses in response to nearby dreams and thoughts, sometimes revealing brief flashes of alternate realities.
