Longsword

Overview

The longsword is a versatile, double-edged blade favored by knights, mercenaries, and Karrnathi militia. Its balance of reach and precision makes it one of the most common martial weapons across Khorvaire.

Appearance

Standard longswords feature a cruciform hilt, a straight 3–4 foot blade, and a leather- or wire-wrapped grip. Styles vary by region and house — Cyran swords tend toward elegance and flourish, while Thrane blades are more austere and functional.

Mechanics

  • Damage: 1d8 slashing
  • Properties: Versatile (1d10)
  • Category: Martial melee weapon
  • Weight: ~3 lbs

Common Variants & Styles

  • Karrnathi Hearthforged – Rough, functional blades with utilitarian balance.
  • Cyran Court – Ornate, dueling-style longswords with carved runes and colored pommels.

Prevalence in Eberron

Widespread. Found in most national armories, adventuring companies, and House Deneith mercenary corps. Especially prominent in the Five Nations.

Related Legendary Weapons

  • Blade of Glory – A famed Thrane longsword wielded by paladins of the Silver Flame.
