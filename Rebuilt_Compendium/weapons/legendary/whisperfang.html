Whisperfang – Legendary Shortbow

← Back to Shortbow

Overview

Crafted by the Valenar elves to hunt giants in silence, Whisperfang is strung with dragon sinew and carved from a petrified tree struck by a Siberys shard.

Properties

  • +2 to attack and damage rolls
  • Ignores all cover except total cover
  • Arrows deal +1d4 psychic damage
  • Grants proficiency in Stealth if not already proficient

Usage & Roleplay

The bow whispers softly when drawn and trembles when lies are spoken nearby.
