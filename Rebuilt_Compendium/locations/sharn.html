Age of Monsters

During the Dhakaani Empire, the area where Sharn now stands was a goblinoid stronghold known as Ja'shaarat. Towering stone keeps and underground passages dominated the landscape. No humans had yet colonized the region.

War of the Mark

In this era, Sharn was a growing city, still reeling from the aftermath of the War of the Mark. Many aberrant-marked refugees hid in the city's depths. The foundations of the upper towers were being laid by Cannith masons and Orien architects.

Modern Era

Today, Sharn stands as the largest city in Khorvaire, a vertical metropolis of magic, intrigue, and industry. Airships dock above while criminal empires thrive below. The scars of the Last War are still fresh in its districts.
