Bestow Curse

Spell Summary

Level3rd
SchoolNecromancy
Casting Time1 action
RangeTouch
ComponentsV, S
DurationConcentration, up to 1 minute
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You touch a creature and bestow a magical curse. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become cursed. Effects include ability disadvantage, attack/defense penalties, wasted turns, or extra damage from the caster.

At Higher Levels

At 5th level or higher, duration becomes 8 hours; at 7th, it becomes 24 hours; at 9th, the curse lasts until dispelled.

Roleplay Manifestation

A dark glyph briefly brands the target’s flesh or shadow. Whispers in forgotten languages echo around them as misfortune begins to weave its threads.
