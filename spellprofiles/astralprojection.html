Description

You and up to eight willing creatures project your astral bodies into the Astral Plane. The physical bodies fall into a state of suspended animation. The silver cord that links astral and physical forms is only severed by certain magical effects or death in the Astral Plane.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

Threads of silver light rise from your heart and stretch outward. Your body slumps peacefully, while your consciousness expands beyond time and space into shimmering astral twilight.
