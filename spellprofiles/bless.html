Bless

Spell Summary

Level1st
SchoolEnchantment
Casting Time1 action
Range30 feet
ComponentsV, S, M (a sprinkling of holy water)
DurationConcentration, up to 1 minute
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You bless up to three creatures of your choice within range. Whenever a target makes an attack roll or a saving throw before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the attack roll or saving throw.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

Roleplay Manifestation

Your words echo with divine conviction. A warm glow outlines your allies, or your deity’s sigil briefly appears above their heads as their blows strike true and their resolve is hardened.
