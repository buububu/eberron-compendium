Description

You create a wall of whirling blades made of magical energy. The wall appears within range and lasts for the duration. When the wall appears, each creature within its area must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 6d10 slashing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success. A creature takes the same damage when entering the wall for the first time on a turn or ending its turn there.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

A shimmering barrier of spinning swords, glyph-etched scimitars, or translucent slivers of radiant force forms in place, humming like a divine buzzsaw of judgment.
