Description

You attempt to suppress strong emotions in a group of people. Targets in a 20-foot-radius sphere make a Charisma save or become indifferent to hostile creatures and resist charm/fright effects while the spell lasts.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

The air thickens like warm fog, and a wave of serenity spreads from the caster. Hearts slow, tensions fade, and even battle cries begin to falter into stillness.
