Description

By casting gem-inlaid bones, cards, or another divination tool, you receive an omen from an otherworldly entity about the results of a specific course of action that you plan to take within the next 30 minutes. The DM chooses from the following possible omens: Weal (good results), Woe (bad results), Weal and Woe (both), or Nothing (neutral or unknowable).

At Higher Levels

This spell does not have an upcast effect.

Roleplay Manifestation

The bones rattle or the cards flare with celestial or eerie light, and the air grows thick with insight as a whisper brushes the edge of your thoughts.
