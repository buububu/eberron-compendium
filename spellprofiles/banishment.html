Banishment

Spell Summary

Level4th
SchoolAbjuration
Casting Time1 action
Range60 feet
ComponentsV, S, M (an item distasteful to the target)
DurationConcentration, up to 1 minute
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You attempt to send one creature that you can see within range to another plane of existence. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be banished. If the target is native to the plane you're on, you banish the target to a harmless demiplane. While there, the target is incapacitated. It returns when the spell ends. If the target is native to a different plane, it is banished back to its home plane. If the spell lasts for the full duration, the target does not return.

At Higher Levels

When cast using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 4th.

Roleplay Manifestation

With a surge of divine energy, a portal flickers into view behind the creature—a swirling void, a divine gate, or the yawning maw of cosmic justice—pulling the target from the world.
