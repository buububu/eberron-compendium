Aid

Spell Summary

Level2nd
SchoolAbjuration
Casting Time1 action
Range30 feet
ComponentsV, S, M (a tiny strip of white cloth)
Duration8 hours
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

Your spell bolsters your allies with toughness and resolve. Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's hit point maximum and current hit points increase by 5 for the duration.

At Higher Levels

When cast using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, a target's hit points increase by an additional 5 for each slot level above 2nd.

Roleplay Manifestation

As you invoke the spell, a soft glow pulses around your allies’ hearts or holy symbols. It may resemble a guardian spirit, divine light, or an ancestral echo, granting courage and vitality.
