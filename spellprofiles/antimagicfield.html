Description

A 10-foot-radius invisible sphere of antimagic surrounds you. Within the sphere, spells can't be cast, summoned creatures disappear, and magical effects are suppressed. The sphere moves with you, centered on you.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

As the spell takes hold, the air around the caster becomes still, like the breath before a storm. Glimmering wards collapse inward, forming an eerie bubble where magic falters and the Weave grows silent.
