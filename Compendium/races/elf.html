Elf

"Long-lived and deeply rooted in tradition, the elves of Eberron span continents, cultures, and ancient power."

Overview

Elves in Eberron are a diverse people whose societies reflect ancient traditions, religious reverence, and martial prowess. From the necromantic reverence of the Aereni to the ancestral valor of the Valenar and the elemental mysticism of the Drow, elves have evolved beyond a single cultural identity.

Culture and Society

Elf culture varies drastically between subraces. The Aereni are scholars and preservers of ancient tradition. The Valenar live as warrior-nomads emulating epic heroes. The Drow in Xen'drik walk spiritual paths, worshipping primal forces or ancient powers.

Major Settlements

Aerenal, the island nation ruled by the Undying Court, is home to the Aereni. The Valenar occupy the Valenar Plains in southeastern Khorvaire. The Drow remain largely in Xen'drik, often within dense jungles or shadowy ruins.

Gameplay Traits

  • Ability Scores: +2 Dexterity
  • Darkvision: 60 feet
  • Keen Senses: Proficiency in the Perception skill
  • Fey Ancestry: Advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep
  • Trance: Elves do not sleep. Instead, they meditate deeply for 4 hours a day
