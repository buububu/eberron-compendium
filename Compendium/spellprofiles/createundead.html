Description

You create undead servants by animating up to three corpses as ghouls. At higher levels, you can create ghasts, wights, or wraiths. They obey your commands for 24 hours unless extended.

At Higher Levels

At 7th level: six ghouls or two ghasts. At 8th: three ghasts or two wights. At 9th: four wights or two wraiths.

Roleplay Manifestation

Darkness swirls as foul energies seep from your focus into dead bodies. They rise jerking and snarling, eyes aglow with necrotic purpose and loyalty to your will.
