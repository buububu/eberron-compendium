Locate Creature

Spell Summary

Level2nd
SchoolDivination
Casting Time1 action
RangeSelf (1,000 feet)
ComponentsV, S
DurationConcentration, up to 1 minute
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You can sense the direction of a creature you are familiar with within range, as long as it is on the same plane of existence.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

A glowing aura hovers in the caster’s hand, directing them toward the location of the creature.

Available To

  • Cleric
