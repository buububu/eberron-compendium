Thunderwave

Spell Summary

Level1st
SchoolEvocation
Casting Time1 action
RangeSelf (15-foot cube)
ComponentsV, S
DurationInstantaneous
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

A wave of thunderous force sweeps out from you. Creatures in the area must make a Con save or take 2d8 thunder damage and be pushed.

Roleplay Manifestation

This spell might manifest according to the caster’s divine or elemental theme: swirling air, crashing thunder, spectral insects, divine radiance, or icy winds depending on their deity or subclass origin.
