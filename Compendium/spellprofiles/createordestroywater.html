Description

You create or destroy up to 10 gallons of water in an open container within range. Alternatively, you can cause rain to fall in a 30-foot cube, extinguishing flames.

At Higher Levels

For every slot level above 1st, you create or destroy 10 additional gallons.

Roleplay Manifestation

You stretch out your hand, and rain forms mid-air or a vapor cloud dissipates. A dry well suddenly brims with water, or a puddle vanishes as if sipped by invisible forces.
