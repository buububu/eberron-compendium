Description

You touch a creature, and for the duration, it can move and act freely even in difficult terrain. It cannot be paralyzed, restrained, or slowed, and the creature ignores difficult terrain.

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

A shimmer of celestial light surrounds the target, making their limbs light and fluid. The aura pulses with divine energy, allowing them to move unfettered and unimpeded.
