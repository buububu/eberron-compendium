Raise Dead

Spell Summary

Level5th
SchoolNecromancy
Casting Time1 hour
RangeTouch
ComponentsV, S, M (a diamond worth 500 gp, which the spell consumes)
DurationInstantaneous
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You touch a dead creature, and it returns to life with 1 hit point. This spell can’t be used to restore a creature that has died of old age, or one who has been dead for more than 10 days.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

A golden light surrounds the target, who shudders and gasps back to life, eyes fluttering open as if drawn from the brink of death.

Available To

  • Cleric
