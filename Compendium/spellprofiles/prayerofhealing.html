Prayer of Healing

Spell Summary

Level2nd
SchoolEvocation
Casting Time10 minutes
Range30 feet
ComponentsV, S
DurationInstantaneous
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You restore 2d8 + your spellcasting modifier hit points to up to six creatures within range. This spell takes 10 minutes to cast, so it can’t be used during combat.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

Divine warmth flows from your hands, like sunlight cascading through clouds. Wounds knit, bruises fade, and the target’s strength returns.

Available To

  • Cleric
