Description

You create 45 pounds of food and 30 gallons of water, enough to sustain fifteen humanoids or five steeds for 24 hours. The food is bland but nourishing, and the water is clean.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

Golden light gathers in your hands, and bread, fruits, or simple grain rations appear in woven baskets or on holy platters. Water flows from the air or from divine springs at your feet.
