Clairvoyance

Spell Summary

Level3rd
SchoolDivination
Casting Time10 minutes
Range1 mile
ComponentsV, S, M (a focus worth at least 100 gp)
DurationConcentration, up to 10 minutes
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You create an invisible sensor within range, in a location familiar or visible. You can hear or see through it as an action. Cannot be detected without see invisibility or true seeing.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

An invisible lens forms in midair. Those attuned to magic feel the faint pull of being watched. The caster’s eyes briefly shimmer like glass, reflecting a place far away.
