Find the Path

Spell Summary

Level6th
SchoolDivination
Casting Time1 minute
RangeSelf
ComponentsV, S, M (a set of divining tools worth 100 gp)
DurationConcentration, up to 1 day
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You divine the shortest, most direct physical route to a location. You must know the location. While concentrating, you are guided without fail toward the place, avoiding dead ends or lost turns.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

Lines of light appear at your feet, like paths marked by prophecy. Each turn and landmark feels intuitively known—as if written into your bones.

Available To

  • Cleric
