Detect Magic

Spell Summary

Level1st
SchoolDivination
Casting Time1 action
RangeSelf (30-foot radius)
ComponentsV, S
DurationConcentration, up to 10 minutes
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

For the duration, you sense the presence of magic within 30 feet. You can see a faint aura and learn the school of magic. Can penetrate most barriers.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

The world pulses with unseen energies—items glow softly, glyphs shimmer, and the caster’s pupils reflect sigils others cannot perceive.

Available To

  • Cleric
