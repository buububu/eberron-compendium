Continual Flame

Spell Summary

Level2nd
SchoolEvocation
Casting Time1 action
RangeTouch
ComponentsV, S, M (ruby dust worth 50 gp)
DurationUntil dispelled
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You touch an object and cause it to glow with a magical flame that sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius. The flame produces no heat and doesn’t use oxygen.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

A holy mark burns gently into the item, glowing with divine or elemental light. It may flicker like a candle, pulse like a heart, or blaze in sacred patterns.

Available To

  • Cleric
