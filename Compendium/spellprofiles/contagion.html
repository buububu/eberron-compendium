Description

You inflict a disease upon a creature. On a failed Constitution save, the target is poisoned. If it fails three saves, it contracts a powerful magical disease like Flesh Rot or Seizure for the duration.

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Your touch blackens flesh or sours blood. The air around you darkens or smells of decay. The affliction spreads like a spiritual toxin.
