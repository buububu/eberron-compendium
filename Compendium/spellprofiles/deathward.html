Death Ward

Spell Summary

Level4th
SchoolAbjuration
Casting Time1 action
RangeTouch
ComponentsV, S
Duration8 hours
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

The first time the target drops to 0 HP, they instead drop to 1 HP. The spell then ends. Alternatively, it prevents one effect that would instantly kill the target.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not benefit from higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

A soft shimmer coats the target’s skin, visible only in flickering light or under divine scrutiny. The warmth of life resists the cold touch of death.

Available To

  • Cleric
