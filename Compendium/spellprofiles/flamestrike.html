Description

A vertical column of divine fire roars down from the heavens. Each creature in a 10-foot radius, 40-foot-high cylinder makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 fire damage and 4d6 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels

When cast using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, damage increases by 1d6 for each type (fire and radiant) per slot level above 5th.

Roleplay Manifestation

A brilliant beam of fire and radiant energy descends like a pillar of divine wrath. The air trembles as heat and holiness merge into one cataclysmic strike.
