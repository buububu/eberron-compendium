Control Weather

Spell Summary

Level8th
SchoolTransmutation
Casting Time10 minutes
Range5 miles
ComponentsV, S, M (burning incense and bits of earth and wood)
DurationConcentration, up to 8 hours
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You take control of the weather within 5 miles of you. The spell changes the current weather conditions (temperature, wind, and precipitation) over the course of 1d4 × 10 minutes. The new conditions persist for the duration unless changed again. You can adjust one category at a time, gradually shifting from one extreme to another.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

Clouds spiral unnaturally, and gusts of wind respond to your gestures. Localized stormfronts gather or dissolve as your divine will is etched across the sky.
