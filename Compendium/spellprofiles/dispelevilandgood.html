Description

You end enchantments, dismiss extraplanar creatures, and protect yourself from certain entities. While the spell lasts, you are protected from aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead, as with protection from evil and good. You can also use your action to touch a creature and attempt to end one of the following effects: - Charm or possession by one of the above types. - Teleport or banish an extraplanar creature if it fails a Charisma saving throw.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher levels.

Roleplay Manifestation

A radiant barrier flares around you like a holy sigil inscribed in light. When you reach to touch others, invisible shackles seem to break, and shrieking echoes vanish into the ether.
