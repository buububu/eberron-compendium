Enhance Ability

Spell Summary

Level2nd
SchoolTransmutation
Casting Time1 action
RangeTouch
ComponentsV, S, M (fur or feather from a beast)
DurationConcentration, up to 1 hour
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You touch a creature and grant it an ability score boost: Bear’s Endurance (Con), Bull’s Strength (Str), Cat’s Grace (Dex), Eagle’s Splendor (Cha), Fox’s Cunning (Int), Owl’s Wisdom (Wis).

At Higher Levels

You can target one additional creature per slot level above 2nd.

Roleplay Manifestation

Animal totems shimmer briefly behind the target. Eyes sharpen, limbs strengthen, and primal instincts hum beneath the skin.

Available To

  • Cleric
