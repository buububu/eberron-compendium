Command

Spell Summary

Level1st
SchoolEnchantment
Casting Time1 action
Range60 feet
ComponentsV
Duration1 round
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You speak a one-word command to a creature. It must succeed on a Wisdom save or follow the command on its next turn: Approach, Drop, Flee, Grovel, Halt, etc.

At Higher Levels

Cast with a higher-level slot to target one additional creature per level above 1st.

Roleplay Manifestation

Your voice booms with divine weight, echoing a single word that resonates in the subject’s mind like a divine edict.

Available To

  • Cleric
