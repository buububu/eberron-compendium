Fire Storm

Spell Summary

Level7th
SchoolEvocation
Casting Time1 action
Range150 feet
ComponentsV, S
DurationInstantaneous
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You create a storm of fire composed of ten 10-foot cubes, arranged as you choose. Each creature in the area makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking 7d10 fire damage on a failed save or half as much on a success. The flames can ignite flammable objects.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

Walls of fire roar up with terrifying precision, shaped by your will. The flames burn white-hot, dancing in sacred runes or forming the blazing shape of a deity’s wrath.
