Description

You step into the Ethereal Plane. For the duration, you move through the Ethereal as if flying, phasing through objects and remaining unseen by creatures on the Material Plane.

At Higher Levels

You can bring additional creatures when casting with higher-level slots (1 creature per level above 7th).

Roleplay Manifestation

Your body glows with misty light before fading like a reflection in fog. Your voice echoes as if from underwater, and your footsteps leave no trace.
