Find Traps

Spell Summary

Level2nd
SchoolDivination
Casting Time1 action
Range120 feet
ComponentsV, S
DurationInstantaneous
SourcePlayer’s Handbook

Description

You sense the presence of any trap within range. A trap is any magical or nonmagical effect intended to inflict sudden or unexpected harm. You don't learn the location—only its presence.

At Higher Levels

This spell does not scale with higher-level slots.

Roleplay Manifestation

Your eyes shimmer with light. Hazardous areas briefly pulse red or hum with alerting resonance—silent warnings others cannot perceive.

Available To

  • Cleric
